{% blocktranslate %} How can the natural environment help fight climate change in Africa? Can Africa play a leading role in the fight against climate change? It is in this context that African Cities Lab and its partners Sèmè City and UM6P-CUS Center of Urban Systems are organizing a webinar to promote integrated urban solutions for African cities to combat biodiversity loss and climate change. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The series of thematic events will provide an online knowledge exchange platform linking policy makers, practitioners, and experts to further integrate biodiversity considerations into cities and development projects. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The webinar will take place in November, with several supporters coming together to learn about climate policy, improve their communication skills, and meet other climate action advocates. Join an even larger online cohort in the fall to hear from expert speakers and virtually connect with dozens of advocates from around the world. {% endblocktranslate %}
