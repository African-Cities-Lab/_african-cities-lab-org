Dr. Martí Bosch

{% blocktranslate %} Doctor in Civil and Environmental Engineering (École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne) {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} I am a Doctor in Civil and Environmental Engineering from the École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). My PhD thesis, undertaken in the CEAT team, focused the spatiotemporal patterns of urbanization and their impacts on the environment and human well being, with a focus on the urban heat island effect. From February to July 2016 I have been intern in the STEEP group of the INRIA Rhone-Alpes, working on a project to evaluate different aspects of Urban Sprawl through the analysis of spatial data obtained from crowd-sourcing sites. This internship served as my Master's Thesis from the Artificial Intelligence and the Web specialization of the Master of Science in Informatics at Grenoble. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} I was a Summer Student in 2015 at the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN), working on a project to visualize the data flows among the several experiment detectors and computing facilities in the center. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} My research career started in February 2014 in an internship in the Tyrex team, in the French institute of Research in Informatics INRIA, that focuses on web data, algorithms and reasoning. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} In October 2014 I received my Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Engineering from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} My areas of interest are Computer Science, Applied Maths and Engineering, more precisely Machine Learning, Optimization and Science and Engineering Simulations. Always willing to learn. {% endblocktranslate %}
