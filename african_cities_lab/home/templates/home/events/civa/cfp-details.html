Call for papers

{% blocktranslate %} ACC 2023 will promote scientific excellence in Africa through the communication and publication of the best research results and works written in original scientific articles in its conference proceedings, its book proceedings and its special issues. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Types of papers" %}

{% blocktranslate %} We wish to stimulate the exchange of new ideas and interdisciplinary perspectives on all themes associated with African cities. To do so, we will accept three different types of papers: {% endblocktranslate %}

  • a. {% blocktranslate %} The complete articles present original and impactful work (6 pages except references) {% endblocktranslate %}
  • b. {% blocktranslate %} Working papers present work in progress or new and visionary ideas (4 pages except references). {% endblocktranslate %}
  • c. {% blocktranslate %} Detailed abstracts present existing and pre-published work (2 pages except references) {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} NB: excess pages will be overcharged at 10 CHF per page and may not exceed 30% of the number of pages required for each type of article. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Presentations & Publications" %}

{% blocktranslate %} All accepted papers will be given a minimum slot for an oral presentation. Depending on the content and quality of the work, papers will be selected for long or short presentations. {% endblocktranslate %}

  • a. {% blocktranslate %} All accepted and presented conference papers will be eligible for publication in our E3S web of conference proceedings indexed in Scopus, Scimago, IET INSPEC, Ei Compendex, EBSCO, Google Scholar, CPCI (Web of Science) (Subject to acceptance - as for all proceedings journals) {% endblocktranslate %}
  • b. {% blocktranslate %} A category of best papers will be published in our proceedings book by Springer book GeoJournal Library (Pending confirmation) indexed in Scopus, Scimago, IET INSPEC, Ei Compendex, EBSCO, Google Scholar, CPCI (Web of Science) (Subject to acceptance - as for all proceedings journals) {% endblocktranslate %}
{% translate "Submission guidelines" %}

{% blocktranslate %} We invite potential authors to send in original work finalized but also work in progress or already published (See article types details). Authors should choose, according to their preference and the requirements of their affiliating institutions, to publish their paper in the E3S web of conference proceeding or in our book to be published by the Springer book GeoJournal Library. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} If you choose to publish your paper in the proceeding E3S web of conference we ask you to follow the instructions of the authors given HERE. Otherwise you use directly one of the templates [Template ACC2023.docx] Word_woc-2col or [Latex template Web of Conferences A4 paper size, two columns format.zip] LATEX_woc-2col depending on the text editor you master. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} If you choose instead to publish your work in the Springer book GeoJournal Library. The manuscript must follow Springer template guidelines. The manuscript must be written using one of the MS Word or LaTeX templates. The maximum number of pages in this case is 12. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Submission" %}

{% blocktranslate %} Once your paper is finalized, you could first send us the pdf version through easychair for evaluation and the source file will then be requested by our committee as needed. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Plagiarism Check" %}

{% blocktranslate %} We remind you once again that a strict plagiarism check will be performed on all papers and will only be tolerated if there is a similarity of max. 15% of all sources + max. 5% of one source. The plagiarism control tool Ithenticate or Turnitin will be used to check the similarity between the papers. {% endblocktranslate %}
